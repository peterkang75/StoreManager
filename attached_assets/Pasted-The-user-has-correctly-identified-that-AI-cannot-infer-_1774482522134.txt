Remove the GPT-4o Triage step completely. The new flow is deterministic:

- **If `FYI_ARCHIVE` or `SPAM_DROP`:** Return 200 OK immediately (Do not trigger AI).
- **If UNKNOWN (No rule exists):** Do NOT trigger any AI. Save the raw email (Subject, Body, senderEmail) into the `NEEDS_ROUTING` queue. Return 200 OK.
